GENERAL QUESTIONS
What time is check in/check out?
Check in time is non-stop, while you can check out until 12am.
Can I check in late in the evening?
We are open 24 hours a day, so there is no problem with you arriving late, as there is always somebody at the reception.
Do you have WiFi?
Yes, we do.
Do you have parking?
Yes, we do.
Do you have a pool?
No, we don't.
Is there daily housekeeping?
Yes, there is. Daily housekeeping is from 7am - 7pm.
Do you allow pets?
Yes, we do allow pets inside the rooms.
Can I smoke in the room?
No, but every room comes with a balcony, so if you have a need for a smoke you can go there.
Is there a safe in the room?
Yes, there is a safe in every room.
Is there a shop in/near the motel?
Yes, there is.
